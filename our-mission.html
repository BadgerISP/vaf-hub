// About Us

Our Purpose

The VAF (Virtual Armed Forces) is a dedicated community of VTOL VR pilots committed to delivering professional-grade flight operations, comprehensive pilot training, and elite squad-based combat tactics. We foster an environment where skill development meets community engagement, creating opportunities for pilots of all experience levels to excel.

Our mission spans three critical pillars: pilot training and education, operational mission execution, and community cohesion. Through structured doctrine, regular training cycles, and coordinated operations, we maintain the highest standards of professionalism and tactical proficiency.

// Organization

How We're Organized

The VAF operates under a structured command hierarchy with three primary components:

T.A.C.S.I.M (Tactical Operations Command, Simulation & Instruction Management) serves as our leadership squadron, overseeing pilot training, operation creation, mission planning, documentation, and managing training programs across all operational squadrons.

Squadron 1 - EU represents our European pilot contingent, coordinating regional operations and training cycles tailored to European pilot availability and preferences.

Squadron 2 - NA represents our North American pilot base, managing operations across North American timezones and leading continental training initiatives.

Each squadron operates with leadership teams responsible for pilot welfare, training progression, and operational execution. Visit our Squadrons page to meet the command structure.
